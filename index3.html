Lab Billing & ICD-10 Cheat Sheet

Nutritional labs (B12, Folate, MMA) face high insurance denial rates without specific medical necessity codes. Ensure one of the following is linked to the lab order:

  • Z79.899 (Long term use of other meds) - Use for patients on Metformin or PPIs.
  • G62.9 (Polyneuropathy, unspecified) - Use if patient has diabetic or unspecified neuropathy.
  • R71.8 (Other abnormal findings of blood) - Gold standard code to guarantee coverage if Macrocytosis (MCV > 100) is present.
  • D64.9 (Anemia, unspecified) - Use during the initial diagnostic workup.
