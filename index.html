Lab Billing & ICD-10 Cheat Sheet

Nutritional labs (B12, Folate, MMA) face high insurance denial rates. Ensure one of the following is linked:

  • Z79.899 (Long term use of other meds) - For patients on Metformin or PPIs.
  • G62.9 (Polyneuropathy, unspecified) - If patient has diabetic or unspecified neuropathy.
  • R71.8 (Other abnormal findings of blood) - Guarantees coverage if Macrocytosis (MCV > 100) is present.
