ICD-10 Billing & Coverage Guide (Point-of-Care)

D64.9 — Anemia, Unspecified Use for initial diagnostic lab pulls (B12, Folate, Iron panel) prior to established etiology. Prevents initial screening denials.
R71.8 — Other Abnormal Blood Chemistry Primary justification code for Macrocytosis (MCV > 100). Guaranteed coverage reflex for Folate, B12, and MMA.
N18.32 / N18.4 — CKD Stage 3b / 4 Primary billing link for Aranesp and IV Iron infusions. Must document ND-CKD status in flowsheet.
E83.110 — Hereditary Hemochromatosis / Overload Use when holding iron for Ferritin > 500 with inflammation to justify therapeutic monitoring labs without infusion.
